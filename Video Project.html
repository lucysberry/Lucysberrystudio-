My video Project

I went through many ideas for this project and in the end did not pull through.
My goal was to create a video that showed the day of a fisherman, getting onto the boat, showing
the scenery, catching the fish, and then heading back. I also wanted
to try to emphasize the repetition that is consistent in a day's work of fishing.
I did not feel inspired by the videos I was finding, and then because
of that, I procrastinated and didn't leave enough time to make this project
cohesive.
