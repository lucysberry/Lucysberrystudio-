Project 2: AI Poster Project

My message I wanted to share through this Anti-AI Poster was that creativity
is drained when relying on AI for creative projects. It drains the
creative flow of a young kid's brain and turns it into AI Slop. I wanted to make
a disturbing image and create it throgh the world of classic art. I chose many
paintings that were surrealist and many others that were your classic white guy European paintings.
I pieced together different things from each painting to create an image of a
mysterious figure breaking into a lady's brain and taking what was inside
of it out. This is supposed to be a metaphor for AI raiding your creativity. Looking back,
I feel I should have made the face younger to make her relatable. The reason I chose that Image
is that I wanted to reference her creativity to be "as fragile as an egg," and that was the
original painting that inspired the whole concept.
